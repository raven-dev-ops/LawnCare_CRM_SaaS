# Roadmap

This roadmap outlines potential enhancements. Timing and scope may change.

## Near Term
- Customer detail enhancements and faster edit flows
- Address autocomplete in customer forms
- Route planning UI improvements (drag-and-drop ordering)
- Weekly schedule interactions and bulk updates

## Mid Term
- Customer portal for service history and invoices
- Automated SMS/email campaigns and reminders
- Route completion notifications (email/SMS)
- Advanced analytics dashboards and exports

## Longer Term
- Real-time crew tracking and live route updates
- Photo uploads for before/after service history
- Weather-aware scheduling suggestions
- Deeper integrations (accounting, marketing, CRM exports)
